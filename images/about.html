MANKEFOR COSMETIC PRODUCTION

MANKEFOR Cosmetic Production began with a simple goal- to provide powerful, affordable, and safe cleaning solutions for homes and businesses.
Inspired by daily cleaning challenges and the need for effective non-skincare products, we started crafting high-quality liquid soaps and detergents.

Our Mission

To enhance cleanliness with reliable and innovative products that serve every household and workspace.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every home in Africa has access to powerful, affordable cleaning products made locally with care.

Our values

  • 100% handcrated with care
  • Gentle yet effective formulas
  • Eco-conscious production
  • Customer satifaction

Why Choose Us?

Unlike other brands, our products are 100% handmade with care, using tested formulas that are safe, eco-conscious, and highly effective.
